Delete Account Request - Apply for E-Wallet application from Shine App

If you would like to delete your E-Wallet account and related data, please send an email request to shineappservice@gmail.com.

Your request should include the email address you used to log in with Google OAuth. Once received, we will verify your identity and proceed to permanently delete your account and all associated data (email, display name, transaction history) within 7 days.

After deletion, your data cannot be restored.
